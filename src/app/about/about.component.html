FAQS

Frequently Ask Question

Where do we meet for pick up?

Please meet your Island Tour Specialist in the lobby at least 15 minutes prior to the tour departure time with your boarding pass or receipt.

Who will our tour guides be?

Our Certified Partners have trained and qualified guides on each tour to ensure that you have a fun and safe experience.

How far in advance do you need to book a tour?

All tours should be booked at least 24 hours in advance.

Will we be refunded if our flight is cancelled?

If your flight is cancelled please contact us and we will reschedule your tour or book a different tour of the same value for another day.

Can I cancel or reschedule my tour when I get to my destination?

Yes you can but please note that the cancellation policy will remain in effect for tours cancelled. Tours can be rescheduled based on availablilty. Please see your Tour Specialist on property or call us for more information.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations Made Easy - Bahamas Travel understands that things don’t always go as planned. That’s why we will do our best to accommodate your needs whenever you want to make a change to your tour or excursion. At Bahamas Travel, we’ve taken every precaution possible to minimize cancellation fees, so you can book with flexibility knowing that we’ve got you covered!
For tours cancelled outside of the following time periods, cancellation fees may be applicable, as follows:
24 hours or less: no refund
48 hours or less: 50% cancellation fee
96 hours or less: 25% cancellation fee
96+ hours: full refund.
